# Hi, I'm Lucan 👋  
# 你好，我是 Lucan 👋

Welcome to my AI-assisted tool lab.  
欢迎来到我的 AI 辅助工具实验室。

I am not a professional software developer. I build small web tools based on real problems from my work, learning, research, and daily life — with the assistance of Codex and other AI coding tools.  
我不是专业软件开发者。我会基于自己在工作、学习、研究和日常生活中的真实问题，借助 Codex 等 AI 编程工具，开发一些轻量级的网页端小工具。

These projects are not meant to be perfect engineering products. They are practical experiments: when I encounter a repeated workflow, a confusing process, or a personal productivity problem, I try to turn it into a simple tool.  
这些项目不追求复杂，也不追求工程化上的完美。它们更像是一组实用实验：当我遇到一个重复流程、一个复杂任务，或者一个具体的小麻烦时，我会尝试把它变成一个可以使用的小工具。

I believe this is one of the most exciting changes of the AI era:  
我相信，这是 AI 时代最有意思的变化之一：

> People with real ideas and real needs can now build their own small tools.  
> 有真实想法和真实需求的人，也可以开始创造自己的小工具。

## About AI-assisted development  
## 关于 AI 辅助开发

Most projects here were developed with the assistance of Codex.  
这里的大多数项目都在 Codex 协助下完成。

The original ideas, use cases, product direction, testing feedback, and iteration logic come from my real personal needs. Codex helps me translate these ideas into working code.  
项目的原始想法、使用场景、产品方向、测试反馈和迭代逻辑，来自我的真实需求。Codex 帮助我把这些想法转化为可运行的代码。

I see AI coding tools not merely as productivity tools, but as a new bridge between ideas and creation.  
我认为，AI 编程工具不仅是提升效率的工具，更是在想法与创造之间架起的一座新桥。

## Why this matters to me  
## 为什么我想做这件事

For a long time, many people had ideas for useful tools, but could not turn them into actual applications because they did not know how to code.  
很长一段时间里，很多人其实都有一些有用的小工具想法，但因为不会写代码，很难真正把它们做出来。

AI coding tools are changing that.  
AI 编程工具正在改变这一点。

Now, a person with real scenarios, clear needs, and a willingness to iterate can start building tools for their own workflow — and maybe help others with similar problems.  
现在，一个有真实场景、明确需求，并且愿意不断迭代的人，也可以开始为自己的工作流创造工具，也有可能帮助到有类似问题的人。

This is my small experiment in that direction.  
这就是我在这个方向上的一个小小实验。
